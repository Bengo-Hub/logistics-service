# Support

Need assistance with the Logistics Service? Here is how to get help.

## Issue Tracker

- Log bugs or enhancements with reproduction steps, request IDs, and severity labels.
- Include tenant ID, task IDs, fleet member IDs, and timestamps where possible.

## Internal Support

- Slack channels:
  - `#bengobox-logistics` – engineering discussions.
  - `#logistics-ops` – operational coordination.
  - `#logistics-incident` – high-severity incident war room.
- PagerDuty/Opsgenie rota documented in the operations runbook.

## External Partners

- Email `support@bengobox.com` with contract reference and SLA tier.
- For critical outages, escalate via your partnership account manager.

## Documentation

- `plan.md` – roadmap and sprint plan.
- `docs/erd.md` – entity relationships and integrations.
- Additional ADRs, API references, and runbooks will reside under `docs/`.

## Service Levels

- Target availability: 99.9%.
- P1 (platform unavailable): respond within 15 minutes, updates every 30 minutes.
- P2 (major degradation): respond within 1 hour, updates every hour.
- P3 (general issues): respond within 1 business day.

Please provide detailed logs and correlation IDs to speed up investigations.

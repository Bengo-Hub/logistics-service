# Code of Conduct

All contributors and maintainers of the Logistics Service are expected to uphold a safe, respectful, and inclusive environment.

## Our Standards

- Use welcoming and professional language.
- Respect differing opinions and experiences.
- Provide constructive feedback and accept criticism gracefully.
- Focus on what is best for the team, riders, and customers.
- Be mindful when discussing sensitive operational incidents.

Unacceptable behaviour includes harassment, discrimination, personal attacks, or sharing confidential information without authorisation.

## Responsibilities

Maintainers are responsible for clarifying the standards and taking appropriate action (warnings, temporary/permanent bans) when violations occur.

## Scope

This policy applies in all project spaces—repositories, issues, pull requests, meetings, chat—and when representing the project in public.

## Reporting

- Email `conduct@bengobox.com` to report incidents.
- Reports are handled confidentially and promptly.

We follow the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org) v2.1.
